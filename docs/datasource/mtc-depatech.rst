###############
About depa.tech
###############
PatZilla uses the `depa.tech api`_ data source to provide professional access to
patent information and fulltext search across DE, EP, WO, US, JP and KR.

.. contents::
   :local:
   :depth: 1

----

.. _mtc-depatech-intro:

************
Introduction
************
The `depa.tech api`_ can be used as a backend for service providers and product developers.
It provides the frontfiles of patent data for the development of applications.

For more detailed information, please refer to their FAQ_ page.

Coverage
========
Descending from DEPAROM_, the coverage of `depa.tech`_ spans
patent documents from offices DE, EP, WO, US, JP and KR.
